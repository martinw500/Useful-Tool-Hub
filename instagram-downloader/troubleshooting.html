Troubleshooting Guide

‚ùå "Please wait a few minutes before you try again"

What it means:

Instagram has temporarily rate-limited our server. This happens when too many requests are made in a short time.

Why it happens:

Our server runs on a cloud platform (Vercel) that shares IP addresses with many other services. Instagram's anti-bot systems detect this and apply stricter limits compared to regular home internet connections.

Solutions:

  • Wait 5-10 minutes and try again
  • Try a different Instagram post URL
  • Come back later - rate limits reset over time

üîå "Cannot connect to backend server"

What it means:

The tool couldn't reach our server to process your request.

Possible causes:

  • Your internet connection is down
  • The server is temporarily offline for maintenance
  • A firewall or browser extension is blocking the request

Solutions:

  • Check your internet connection
  • Try disabling ad blockers or privacy extensions
  • Wait a few minutes and try again

üîí "No media found in post"

What it means:

The tool couldn't find any images or videos in the Instagram post.

Possible causes:

  • The post is private or has been deleted
  • The URL is incorrect or incomplete
  • The post contains only text or polls (no media)
  • The account is private and you need to be logged in to view it

Solutions:

  • Verify the URL is correct (should look like: instagram.com/p/ABC123...)
  • Make sure the post is public
  • Try a different post

  • Story URLs
  • IGTV URLs (old format)
